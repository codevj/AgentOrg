# Task: Evaluate European Market Expansion

## Problem

Revenue growth in North America is slowing (8% YoY vs. 22% last year). The board is asking whether we should expand to Europe. We need a structured analysis of the opportunity, risks, and a clear recommendation.

## Appetite

Large — full strategy team with iteration. This decision has significant resource implications.

## Solution

Analyze the European market across key dimensions: market size, competitive landscape, regulatory requirements (GDPR, AI Act), go-to-market options (direct vs. partnership), and resource requirements. Produce 2-3 strategic options with tradeoffs and a clear recommendation.

## Rabbit Holes

- Do not boil the ocean — focus on UK, Germany, and France as the initial three markets
- Do not get lost in regulatory details — summarize at the decision-maker level
- Avoid analysis paralysis — three options is the max

## No-gos

- No financial projections beyond 18 months — too speculative
- No recommendation to "do more research" — the point is to make a decision
- No comparison with Asia-Pacific — that is a separate analysis

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Market size estimated for UK, Germany, France
- [ ] Top 3 competitors per market identified
- [ ] Regulatory summary at decision-maker level
- [ ] 2-3 mutually exclusive strategic options with pros/cons
- [ ] Clear recommendation with justification
- [ ] Action plan with timeline if recommendation is "go"
