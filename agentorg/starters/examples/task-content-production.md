# Task: Write a Blog Post on AI Agent Organizations

## Problem

Our blog has no content explaining the concept of AI agent organizations — teams of AI roles working together on tasks. This is our core product concept and potential customers have no educational resource to understand it.

## Appetite

Medium — full content team. Needs research, writing, editing, and fact-checking.

## Solution

Write a 1500-2000 word blog post that explains what AI agent organizations are, why they produce better output than single-agent approaches, and how someone would set one up. Use concrete examples from at least 3 different domains (not just software).

## Rabbit Holes

- Do not make this a product tutorial — it should be educational, not promotional
- Do not compare specific AI tools or frameworks — keep it concept-level
- Avoid jargon — write for a smart generalist, not an AI engineer

## No-gos

- No pricing or feature announcements
- No claims about AI capabilities without citation
- No "AI will replace jobs" framing — focus on empowerment

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 1500-2000 words
- [ ] Examples from 3+ domains
- [ ] Every factual claim has a source
- [ ] Readability score above grade 8 level
- [ ] Consistent with brand voice (clear, direct, no hype)
