# Task: Research the State of AI Agent Frameworks in 2026

## Problem

We need to understand the current landscape of AI agent frameworks to inform our product positioning. The space is moving fast and our last analysis is 6 months old.

## Appetite

Medium — full research team. Needs careful question framing, thorough research, critical synthesis, and skeptical review.

## Solution

Produce a synthesis of the top 10 AI agent frameworks/tools, their core abstractions, adoption signals, and where the market is heading. Focus on what has changed in the last 6 months and what trends are emerging.

## Rabbit Holes

- Do not catalog every framework — focus on the top 10 by adoption/influence
- Do not write a tutorial for each framework — summarize at the strategic level
- Avoid recency bias — distinguish between hype and genuine traction

## No-gos

- No product recommendations — this is research, not a buying decision
- No internal strategy implications — that is a separate analysis
- No speculative predictions beyond 12 months

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Top 10 frameworks identified with adoption signals
- [ ] Core abstraction and differentiation for each
- [ ] 3-5 emerging trends with evidence
- [ ] Information gaps explicitly noted
- [ ] Every claim sourced
